Privacy Policy for Tib

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Tib ("the app," "we") is a macOS menu-bar app that helps you track tasks, assignments, and deadlines. We built Tib to be privacy-respecting: your data stays on your device wherever possible, and we don't sell it or use it for advertising.

What Tib stores on your device

Your tasks, assignments, notes, to-dos, tags, and reminder settings are stored locally on your Mac. If you connect Canvas, your Canvas access token or feed URL is stored securely in the macOS Keychain on your device and is used only to fetch your assignments directly from your school's Canvas server. We never receive or store it. Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your Mac.

Calendar access

If you choose to export assignments to Apple Calendar, Tib adds those events to your calendar on your device. Tib does not read or upload your existing calendar data.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions and access codes are handled through RevenueCat and Apple. To manage subscription status, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated anonymous app user ID and purchase details from Apple; this is not linked to your name or email. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy. Apple processes all payments per Apple's privacy policy.

Access codes

If you redeem a friends-and-family access code, the app sends the code and your anonymous app user ID to our redemption service to grant access. No personal information is collected.

What we do NOT collect

We do not require an account, name, or email to use Tib. We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not serve ads. We do not sell or share your personal data.

Children

Tib is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
